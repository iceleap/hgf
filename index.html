Hazel grey farms

Grown in the Yakima Valley, Washington

LOYAL TO THE SOIL AND THE NUTRITION IT PROVIDES

We are a certified organic small-scale farm nestled in the foothills of West Valley Yakima, Washington. Our intentions are to produce the highest quality vegetables and herbs. We accomplish this while restoring and enhancing soil quality with regenerative practices that are inline with our natural habitats and environment on and around our farm.

Locally sourced and freshness makes all the difference

Product availability to the local establishments and our community are key for nutritious and high quality produce that come fresh and direct from our soil to your family in a shorter period of time.

Benefit of direct farm buying

The understanding of where and how your food is grown while building a relationship with the farmer who knows the importance of a premium quality product.
